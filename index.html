About Mzansi Auto

Our Story

South Africa's Trusted
Auto Parts Supplier

Mzansi Auto was founded with a single mission: to make quality auto parts accessible to every South African — whether you're a mechanic, panel beater, or private vehicle owner.

We stock parts for German, Japanese and Chinese vehicles — brands that dominate South African roads. From a BMW 3 Series to a Toyota Hilux to a Haval Jolion, we've got you covered with new, used, OEM and aftermarket options.

Every part in our inventory is graded and described honestly. We don't list parts we wouldn't put in our own vehicles.

🔄 Returns Policy

We offer a 30-day return policy on all parts, subject to the following conditions:

  • Part must be in original condition — unused, uninstalled
  • Original packaging must be intact where applicable
  • Proof of purchase required
  • Wrong part supplied: we cover return shipping
  • Change of mind: customer covers return shipping
  • Electrical parts are non-returnable once installed
